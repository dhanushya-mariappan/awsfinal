Frequently Asked Questions

What is Amazon DSP (Demand-Side Platform)? +
Amazon DSP is a demand-side platform that allows you to programmatically buy display, video, and audio ads both on and off Amazon. It uses Amazon's exclusive first-party data to reach audiences based on their actual purchase behavior.
Do I need to sell products on Amazon to use the DSP? +
No. Amazon DSP is available to both endemic (those who sell on Amazon) and non-endemic advertisers (those who don't). Brands in sectors like Real Estate, BFSI, and Automobile can leverage Amazon's audience data to drive traffic to their own websites.
How does Amazon Marketing Cloud (AMC) differ from standard reporting? +
Standard reporting provides aggregate metrics on campaign performance. AMC is a privacy-safe, cloud-based clean room that allows for custom, cross-channel analysis, helping you understand the full customer journey and the hidden value of your ad spend.
What is the minimum budget for an Amazon DSP campaign? +
While Amazon's managed service typically requires higher minimums, working with a partner like us allows for more flexible entry points. However, to see statistically significant results from the platform's machine learning, we recommend a consistent monthly spend.
Can I target specific audiences on Prime Video? +
Yes. Prime Video and Streaming TV ads allow you to target audiences using Amazon’s first-party signals, such as lifestyle interests, in-market segments, and demographic data, ensuring your premium video content reaches the right viewers.
